Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Directory ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our website and services, including submitting and removing AI tools from our directory.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Tool Submission Information: Basic information (tool name, description), features, tags, listing type (free or paid), and contact details.
  • Removal Requests: Tool identifier, ownership verification data (e.g., email or website match), and reason for removal.
  • Contact Data: Information submitted through the Contact Us page or support emails, such as your name and email address.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and other usage data collected via cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To list, publish, update, or remove AI tools based on your submissions.
  • To communicate with you regarding tool listings or support inquiries.
  • To verify ownership during tool removal requests.
  • To process featured or premium listing payments (if applicable).
  • To improve the website, monitor usage, and enhance user experience.

3. Tool Ownership and Responsibility

Users are responsible for the accuracy, legality, and originality of any tool information they submit. We reserve the right to remove or reject submissions that violate our guidelines.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to track site usage. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

5. Payment Information

Payments for promoted listings are handled securely through third-party providers (such as Stripe or PayPal). We do not store or process payment card details directly.

6. Data Retention

Submitted tool data is retained for as long as it is listed. Contact and removal data is retained only as necessary for verification, support, and audit purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction or deletion of your submitted tool.
  • Request a copy of the data you've submitted.
  • Withdraw your submission at any time by providing verification.

8. Contact and Support

If you have questions or concerns about this policy or your data, you can:

  • Use our Contact Us page
  • Email us at: support@ai-directory.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
